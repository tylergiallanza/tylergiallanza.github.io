Tyler Giallanza

Graduate Student at Princeton University

I am currently a graduate Student in Psychology and Neuroscience at Princeton University, advised by Jonathan Cohen. I'm broadly interested in understanding how humans apply knowledge learned in one context to new situations. This cross-domain transfer is a key reason humans can perform well in a breadth of domains and learn new things quickly. I investigate the role that episodic memory plays in this process, and how it interacts with other cognitive processes like attention and semantic memory. I use a combination of behavioral experiments and computational modeling to address these questions.
