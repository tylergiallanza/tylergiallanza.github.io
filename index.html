About Me

I'm a final-year undergraduate studying Computer Science at SMU. Currently, I'm abroad at the University of Oxford until June 2020. I'm looking to join a cognitive neuroscience lab for a PhD starting in Fall 2020.

Research Interests

I'm interested in using applied machine learning to develop mechanistic explanations for cognitive processes in humans. I'm currently working with Drs. Jonathan Cohen and Marius Cătălin Iordan at the Princeton Neuroscience Institute on understanding how humans represent semantic knowledge. We use a combination of computational modeling, behavioral experiments, and fMRI studies.
